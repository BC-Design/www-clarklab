Luis P. Fonseca is an Assistant Professor of Department of Chemical Engineering at the Instituto Superior Técnico (IST) – Technical University of Lisbon (UTL).  He received his B.S. in Chemical Engineering (1983), M.Sc. in Biochemical Engineering (1989) and Ph.D. in Biotechnology (1995) from the IST - UTL.

His academic duties (1989/2004) have been on Fermentation Technology, Enzyme Technology and Biochemical Industry Project. His research interests, as well as researcher of Faculty Staff at BioEngineering Research Group-BERG have been in Fermentation and monitoring, Isolationand purification of proteins, Process Integration, and Biocatalysis and Biosensors.

Meanwhile, he was Lieutenant of Portuguese Navy (1983-1985), Chemical Engineer, Hovione, Lda (1985-1986), Visiting Researcher during M.Sc. project in CIPAN, Lda (1987-1988) and Visiting Researcher during Pos-Doc project in Enzyme Biotechnology Group at University of Leeds, UK (1998-1999). Currently, Luis P. Fonseca is a Visiting Scholar at University of California, at Berkeley in the Department of Chemical Engineering in the Prof. Douglas Clark´s Group.
