Research Interests: Genomic Expre3ssion Analysis of Extremophiles

My research involves growing the extremophile Methanococcus Jannaschii.  This strict-anaerobic archaeon thrives in temperatures of 90C and pressures up to 750 atm.  I will be using a high temperature, high pressure reactor to cultivate cells.  Using DNA microarrays to study the gene expression profiles at different pressures, we will be able to delve into the origins of life.

My daily work involve preparing media for cell cultivation, operating the high temperature, high pressure reactor, RNA extraction, and some glassware cleaning.
