Luis Fonseca

Visiting Professor

Ph.D. Biotechnology, 1995, Instituto Superior Técnico (IST) – Technical University of Lisbon (UTL)

M.Sc. Biochemical Engineering, 1989, IST-UTL

B.S. Chemical Engineering, 1983, IST-UTL

 

Research Interests: Biotechnology with a particular interest in Fermentation Technology and Monitoring, Isolation and Purification of Proteins, Process Integration, and Biocatalysis and Biosensors.

Luis P. Fonseca is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Chemical Engineering at IST-UTL (1995/2004) as well as Faculty Researcher at the BioEngineering Research Group-BERG. Prior positions include to this a Lieutenant of Portuguese Navy (1983-1985), a Chemical Engineer at Hovione, Lda (1985-1986), a Visiting Researcher during M.Sc. project in CIPAN, Lda (1987-1988), Teaching Professor at IST-UTL (1989-1995) and a Visiting Researcher for a Post-Doctoral project in the Enzyme Biotechnology Group at the University of Leeds, UK (1998-1999). Currently, Luis P. Fonseca is a Visiting Scholar in Professor Douglas Clark’s research group at the University of California, at Berkeley.
